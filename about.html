The name Maysaysay is a play on three Filipino words.
The first is 'may,' a shortened form of 'mayroon' meaning "there is." The second is 'saysay' which means "to make sense." The third is 'kasaysaysan' which translates to 'history.' Together, Maysaysay means "There is sense in history."

MISSION
Maysaysay collects and delivers information about Philippine history in a way that resonates with Filipino youth. By spotlighting underrecognized stories of the past, learners are given another perspective by which to understand the present. Maysaysay takes advantage of audio-visual elements to keep them interested in learning about their history as Filipinos and an online platform to reach as many learners as possible.
VISION
Maysaysay aims to contribute in the preservation of Philippine culture and history by enriching the learning processes of Filipino youth to create a progressive society within the country.
